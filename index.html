⭐ POKÉMON ADVENTURE ⭐

🎮 Game Controle

MOVEMENT

W - Move Up

A - Move Left

S - Move Down

D - Move Right

INTERACTIONS

SPACE - Talk to NPCs / Continue dialogue

CLICK - Start game audio

HOW TO PLAY

🗺️ Explore the town: Move around using WASD keys

💬 Talk to Villagers: Walk up to NPCs and press SPACE

⚔️ Battle wild monsters: Walk through grass (darker areas)

⚔️ During battles: Click attack buttons to fight

⚔️ Track health: Watch the green health bars

BATTLE SYSTEM

. Random encounters occur in tall grass (1% change per step)

. Choose attacks by clicking buttons

. Each attack has different damage and effects

. Reduce enemy health to 0 to win

. Click dialogue box to continue battle messages

PARTY SYSTEM

👥 Start a battle: You'll see your 3 party members on the right side

🔄 During battle: Press 1, 2, or 3 to switch monsters

💀 When a monster faints: Click dialogue or press a number to switch to another

📈 Track progress: Each monster levels independently and remembers their stats

⚡ Experience: Win battles to gain 30 XP per monster (Level up at 100 XP)

TIPS

🌟 Stay away from red grass if you want to avoid battles

🌟 Talk to everyone - they might have useful info

🌟 Watch attack types and colors when choosing moves
